Jack Rose

Ingredients

  • 2 oz apple brandy
  • ½ oz lime juice
  • ¾ oz grenadine (or less)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain into a glass. (Shaking is preferred, but building in a rocks glass over ice works similarly.)
