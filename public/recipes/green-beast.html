Green Beast

Ingredients

  • 1 oz absinthe (Pernod)
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 4 oz water
  • 4 cucumber slices, plus more for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a pitcher or glass with ice.
  2. Stir, and garnish with cucumber slices.
