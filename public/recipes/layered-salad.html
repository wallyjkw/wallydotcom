Layered Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head lettuce, shredded
  • 1 medium white onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 can peas, drained
  • Bacon bits
  • 1 cup mayo
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 packet dry ranch dressing mix
  • Extra-sharp cheddar, shredded

Steps

  1. Shred the lettuce into a 9×13 dish.
  2. Add a layer of thinly sliced onion.
  3. Add the drained peas.
  4. Sprinkle on bacon bits.
  5. Stir together the mayo, sour cream, and ranch packet, then spread over the top.
  6. Finish with a layer of shredded extra-sharp cheddar.
