Taco Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 8 tsp chili powder
  • 6 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.
