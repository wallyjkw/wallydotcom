Maple Jalapeño Bourbon

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ½ oz maple syrup
  • 8 half-moon jalapeño slices, plus 5 seeds
  • ¼ lemon, squeezed
  • Long jalapeño strips and a lemon slice, for garnish

Steps

  1. Muddle the jalapeño slices and seeds with the maple syrup.
  2. Squeeze in the lemon, add the bourbon, and stir over ice.
  3. Fine-strain onto fresh ice.
  4. Garnish with long jalapeño strips and a lemon slice.
