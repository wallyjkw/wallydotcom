Pontoon Smash

Batch for a group — makes 6.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz bourbon
  • 1 oz absinthe
  • 4½ oz lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 6 oz pineapple juice
  • 3 oz simple syrup
  • 25 mint leaves
  • 6 mint crowns (one per glass)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker and shake hard on ice for 6–8 seconds. No need to muddle the mint — the ice will “smash” it for you.
  2. Fine-strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a mint sprig — and if you're feeling festive, a pineapple slice or leaf.

Notes

  • Makes 6 — about 26.5 oz total, plus ice for shaking.
  • Volume reference: 128 oz / gallon · 64 oz / half gallon · 32 oz / quart.
