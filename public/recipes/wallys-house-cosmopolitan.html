Wally's House Cosmopolitan

Ingredients

  • 1½ oz Tito's vodka
  • 1 oz Cointreau or Drillaud orange liqueur
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • ½ oz fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake hard for 10–15 seconds.
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.
  4. Optional: garnish with a lime wheel or expressed orange peel.
