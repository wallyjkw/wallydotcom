Verte Chaud

Ingredients

  • 1 oz green Chartreuse
  • 6 oz hot chocolate
  • Marshmallows, to top

Steps

  1. Make the hot chocolate and pour into a mug.
  2. Stir in the green Chartreuse.
  3. Top with marshmallows.

Notes

  • "Green hot" — a warm one for cold nights.
