Whiskey Smash

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ¾ oz lemon juice (or lime)
  • ½–¾ oz simple syrup
  • 4 mint leaves
  • 1 lemon peel
  • Mint sprig, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, mint leaves, and lemon peel to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake, then strain over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a mint sprig.
