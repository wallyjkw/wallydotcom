Algonquin

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rye
  • 1 oz pineapple juice
  • ¼ oz grenadine

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain into a coupe.

Notes

  • The classic Algonquin doesn't use grenadine, but many bartenders add just a touch — this is that variation.
