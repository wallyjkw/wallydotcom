Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Angostura bitters, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a shaker and dry shake (no ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again, then strain into a glass.
  3. Garnish with a few drops of Angostura bitters on the foam.
