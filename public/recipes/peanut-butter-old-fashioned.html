Peanut Butter Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 1½ oz Skrewball peanut butter whiskey
  • 1 oz rye
  • 4 dashes chocolate bitters
  • 1 large ice cube

Steps

  1. Add the Skrewball, rye, and chocolate bitters to a rocks glass.
  2. Add the large ice cube and stir to chill.
