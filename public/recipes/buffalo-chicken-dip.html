Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 block (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup Frank's RedHot sauce
  • ½ cup blue cheese or ranch dressing
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1–2 cups cooked chicken, chopped or shredded

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Bake in a 350°F oven for 30 minutes, or until bubbly around the edges.

Notes

  • Chicken: usually 1 large chicken breast, 2 smaller ones, or whatever leftover cooked chicken is around.
