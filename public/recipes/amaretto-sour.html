Amaretto Sour

Ingredients

  • 1½ oz amaretto
  • ¾ oz cask-proof bourbon
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 1 tsp rich simple syrup (2:1)
  • ½ oz egg white
  • Lemon peel and cherries, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker and dry shake (no ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again, then strain over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a lemon peel and brandied cherries.

Notes

  • Rating: B.
