Rattlesnake

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rye
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp absinthe (or rinse the glass with absinthe)

Steps

  1. Add the rye, lemon, simple syrup, egg white, and absinthe to a shaker and dry shake (no ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again.
  3. Strain into a glass (rinsed with absinthe instead, if you prefer).

Notes

  • Rating: B.

Source
