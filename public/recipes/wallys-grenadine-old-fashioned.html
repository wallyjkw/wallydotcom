Wally's Grenadine Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ½ tsp grenadine (instead of simple syrup)
  • 1 dash black walnut bitters
  • 1 dash orange bitters
  • 1 large ice cube
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the bourbon, grenadine, black walnut bitters, and orange bitters to a rocks glass.
  2. Add the large ice cube and stir to chill.
  3. Express an orange peel over the top and drop it in.
