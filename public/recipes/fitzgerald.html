Fitzgerald

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Lemon peel, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain into a coupe and garnish with a lemon peel.
