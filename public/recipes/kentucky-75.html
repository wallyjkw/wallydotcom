Kentucky 75

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rye
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Chilled Prosecco, to top

Steps

  1. Add the rye, lemon, simple syrup, and bitters to a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain into a glass and top with chilled Prosecco.
  3. Give a gentle stir.
