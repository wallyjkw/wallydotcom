Godfather

Ingredients

  • 2¼ oz whiskey (scotch, Irish, or bourbon)
  • ½ oz amaretto
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 1 large ice cube
  • Orange peel, expressed

Steps

  1. Add the whiskey, amaretto, and bitters to a rocks glass.
  2. Add the large ice cube and stir to chill.
  3. Express the orange peel over the drink.
