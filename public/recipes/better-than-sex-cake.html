Better Than Sex Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box cake mix
  • 3 eggs
  • ¾ cup water
  • ¾ cup oil
  • 8 oz sour cream or yogurt
  • 6 oz mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix well and pour into a greased Bundt pan.
  2. Bake at 350°F for 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  3. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, or pour the glaze on top.

Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • A little milk or cream
  • A little melted butter

Mix together and pour over the cake.
