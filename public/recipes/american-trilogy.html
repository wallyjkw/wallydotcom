American Trilogy

Ingredients

  • 1 oz rye
  • 1 oz apple brandy
  • ¼ oz demerara syrup
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Steps

  1. Stir with ice.
  2. Serve over a big ice cube.
  3. Garnish with an expressed orange peel.
