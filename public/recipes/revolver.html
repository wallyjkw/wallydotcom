Revolver

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ½ oz coffee liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • Orange peel, flamed (or regular), for garnish

Steps

  1. In a mixing glass with ice, combine the bourbon, coffee liqueur, and orange bitters.
  2. Stir until well chilled, then strain into a chilled coupe.
  3. Garnish with a flamed (or regular) orange peel.
