Revolver

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ½ oz coffee liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1 large ice cube
  • Orange peel, flamed (or regular), for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a rocks glass over a large piece of ice and stir.
  2. Garnish with a flamed orange peel or a regular orange peel.
