Pink Lady

Ingredients

  • 1½ oz gin
  • ½ oz apple brandy
  • ½ oz lemon juice (or lime)
  • ¾ oz grenadine (or less)
  • 1 egg white

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker and dry shake (no ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again.
  3. Strain into a coupe.

Notes

  • Meh — try with a better grenadine.
