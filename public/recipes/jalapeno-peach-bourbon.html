Jalapeño Peach Bourbon

Ingredients

  • 4 oz bourbon
  • 1 peach
  • 2 oz jalapeño syrup
  • Peach slice and jalapeño slice, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain thin onto fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a peach slice and a jalapeño slice.

Notes

  • Rating: C. Not as good as hoped — a splash of pineapple helped, but still not as good as the pineapple drink.
