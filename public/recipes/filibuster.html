Filibuster

Ingredients

  • 1½–2 oz rye
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz maple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Angostura bitters and a lemon peel, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the rye, lemon, maple syrup, and egg white to a shaker and dry shake (no ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again, then strain.
  3. Garnish with Angostura on top and an expressed lemon peel.

Notes

  • A rye sour with maple syrup.
