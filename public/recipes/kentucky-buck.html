Kentucky Buck

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz ginger syrup
  • 1 strawberry
  • 1–2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • About 2 oz soda water
  • Half strawberry, lemon wheel, or mint sprig, for garnish

Steps

  1. Muddle the strawberry in the bottom of a shaker.
  2. Add the bourbon, lemon, ginger syrup, and bitters; shake for six to eight seconds.
  3. Strain over fresh ice in a tall glass and top with soda water.
  4. Garnish with a half strawberry, a lemon wheel, a mint sprig — or all three.
