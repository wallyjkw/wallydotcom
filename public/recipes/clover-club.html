Clover Club

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup (or raspberry syrup)
  • 3–5 raspberries
  • 1 egg white
  • Lemon peel and raspberries, for garnish

Steps

  1. Muddle the raspberries with the syrup in a shaker.
  2. Add the gin, lemon juice, and egg white; dry shake (no ice).
  3. Add ice, shake again, and double-strain into a coupe.
  4. Express a lemon peel over the foam and garnish with raspberries.

Notes

  • Basically a gin sour. Rating: A.
