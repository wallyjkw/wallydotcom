Benton's Bacon Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bacon-infused bourbon (see below)
  • ¼ oz maple syrup
  • 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the bacon-infused bourbon, maple syrup, and bitters to a glass with ice.
  2. Stir.
  3. Express an orange peel over the drink and drop it in as garnish.

Bacon-Infused Bourbon

  • 8 oz bourbon
  • 1 Tbsp non-scorched bacon fat (liquid)
  1. Combine and let sit at room temperature for ~7 hours.
  2. Freeze ~2 hours.
  3. Filter until clear.

Notes

  • Freaking awesome!
  • Bourbons tried: Early Times was the best — better than Kirkland 120 proof, and better than Sazerac.
  • To infuse a full 750 ml bottle (≈25.36 oz), use 3+ Tbsp bacon fat.
