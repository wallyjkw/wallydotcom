Old Fashioned

Cocktail · serves 1 · 5 min

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon (or rye)
  • 1 sugar cube (or 1 tsp simple syrup)
  • 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Splash of water
  • Orange peel, to garnish

Steps

  1. Muddle the sugar cube with the bitters and a splash of water in a rocks glass.
  2. Add a large ice cube and the bourbon.
  3. Stir gently until chilled, about 30 seconds.
  4. Express the orange peel over the top, then drop it in.
