Apple Cider Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1 oz apple cider
  • ¼ oz maple syrup
  • Apple slice or cinnamon stick, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the bourbon, apple cider, and maple syrup to a rocks glass with ice.
  2. Stir to chill.
  3. Garnish with an apple slice or a cinnamon stick.

Notes

  • Amounts are a starting point — adjust the cider and maple to taste.
