WhistlePig S'mores Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 1½ oz CampStock Wheat Whiskey (rye is better)
  • ¼ oz WhistlePig barrel-aged maple syrup
  • 3 dashes chocolate bitters
  • Toasted marshmallow, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the whiskey, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters to a rocks glass with ice.
  2. Stir to chill.
  3. Garnish with a toasted marshmallow.

Notes

  • Rating: B. Rye works better than the wheat whiskey.
