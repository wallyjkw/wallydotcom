Red Raider

Ingredients

  • 1¾ oz bourbon
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz Cointreau
  • ½ oz grenadine
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously for six to eight seconds.
  2. Strain over ice into a rocks glass.
  3. Garnish with an orange peel (a lemon peel or wheel works too, or skip it).

Notes

  • Use a spicier bourbon at 45% ABV or higher, like Bulleit or Elijah Craig Small Batch. For anything below 45%, bump the bourbon up to 2 oz.
  • Reach for a quality grenadine that lists pomegranate, such as Liquid Alchemist or Small Hand Foods.
  • Cointreau is essential here — substitutes like Grand Marnier don't come out the same.
