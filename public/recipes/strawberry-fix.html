Strawberry Fix

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup (jalapeño syrup is great)
  • 2–3 strawberries
  • Jalapeño half-moon, for garnish

Steps

  1. Smash the strawberries in a shaker.
  2. Add the rest with crushed ice and shake for six to eight seconds.
  3. Pour unstrained into the glass and garnish with a jalapeño half-moon.

Notes

  • Rating: A with jalapeño syrup.
  • Batch: 12 strawberries (mashed), 4 lemons, 16 oz bourbon, 6 oz jalapeño syrup. Add crushed ice and shake/stir.
