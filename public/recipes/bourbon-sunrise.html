Bourbon Sunrise

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz grenadine
  • Splash of soda

Steps

  1. Add the bourbon, lemon juice, and grenadine to a glass with ice.
  2. Top with a splash of soda.

Notes

  • Not the tequila sunrise. Very refreshing — a pool drink.
