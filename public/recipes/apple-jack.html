Apple Jack

Ingredients

  • 2 oz apple brandy
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup
  • 1 oz apple cider

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake, then strain.

Notes

  • Smaller-batch ratio also works: 1½ oz apple brandy, ½ oz lemon, ½ oz simple syrup, ½ oz apple cider.
