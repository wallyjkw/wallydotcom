Pineapple Jalapeño Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 1½ oz pineapple juice
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz jalapeño syrup
  • Jalapeño slice, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain onto fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a jalapeño slice.

Notes

  • Rating: B.
