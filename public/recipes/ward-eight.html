Ward Eight

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rye (or bourbon)
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz orange juice
  • ½ oz homemade grenadine

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake, then strain into a chilled coupe.

Notes

  • From the early 1900s — drinks like a cross between a Whiskey Sour and the Red Raider.
