Mint Julep

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • ½ oz simple syrup
  • Mint leaves, plus a sprig for garnish

Steps

  1. Rub the mint leaves around the inside of the glass.
  2. Add the bourbon and simple syrup with crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with a mint sprig.

Notes

  • Use more mint than just the rub.
  • Try with jalapeño syrup, or add a splash of club soda.
