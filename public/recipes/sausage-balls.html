Sausage Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sausage
  • 1 bar cheddar, shredded
  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix all ingredients together until evenly combined.
  3. Roll into 1-inch balls and arrange on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, until golden and cooked through.
