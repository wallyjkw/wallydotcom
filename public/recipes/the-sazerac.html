The Sazerac

Ingredients

  • 2½ oz rye
  • ¼ oz simple syrup
  • ~5 dashes Peychaud's bitters
  • Absinthe, to rinse the glass
  • Lemon peel, for expressing

Steps

  1. Rinse a chilled glass with absinthe, coating the inside, and discard the excess.
  2. In a mixing glass with ice, combine the rye, simple syrup, and Peychaud's bitters.
  3. Stir, then strain into the absinthe-rinsed chilled glass.
  4. Express the lemon peel over the drink last.

Notes

  • Doubled (two drinks): 5 oz rye, ½ oz (3 tsp) simple syrup, 8 dashes Peychaud's.
